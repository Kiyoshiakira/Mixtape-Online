Upcoming Features

🎮 VR Experience

The heart of My Mixtape - a fully immersive VR environment where you can:

  • Walk up to your virtual VHS shelf
  • Physically grab and examine tape cases
  • Insert tapes into a realistic VCR with authentic sounds
  • Watch content on a virtual TV or desktop screen
  • Customize your virtual room and shelving
📱

Mobile Remote Control

iOS and Android app that acts as your universal remote. Browse playlists, control playback, and manage your collection from anywhere in the room.

⏰

Smart Timers

Set custom timers for movies and shows. Choose from 25min, 50min presets, or set custom durations. We're working on intelligent duration detection from IMDb and Wikipedia!

🌐

Universal URL Support

Works with any streaming service - Netflix, Hulu, YouTube, Disney+, and more. If it has a URL, it can be part of your mixtape.

🎨

Custom Tape Labels

Design your own VHS labels with custom artwork, colors, and text. Make each tape uniquely yours, just like the old days.
