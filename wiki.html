‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a VR headset to use My Mixtape?

No! While the full experience is designed for VR, My Mixtape also works on desktop, with controls on phones and tablets as well. The VR experience is the premium way to enjoy the app, but you can create your mixtapes on any device.

Which streaming services are supported?

Currently, we support all streaming services that take you the video with a direct link.

Is My Mixtape free to use?

My Mixtape offers a free tier with basic features and limited VR environments. Premium subscriptions unlock additional environments, advanced features, and unlimited mixtape creation.

Can I use my existing playlists?

Yes! My Mixtape can import your existing playlists from .txt files and convert them into a mixtape.

How do I share my mixtapes?

You can share mixtapes through the app's social features, generate shareable links, or export them back to your streaming service as regular playlists.

What makes this different from regular streaming apps?

My Mixtape focuses on the set top box complete with VCR experience of TV watching. It's about family nostalgia, friends watching their favorite shows during sleepovers, that comfortable feeling of the relaxing TV keeping you company while you're sick, curation, and creating memorable moments with your friends again, enhanced by immersive VR environments.
