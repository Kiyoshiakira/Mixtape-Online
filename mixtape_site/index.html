What is mymixtape.online?

It's a platform for creating, sharing, and experiencing personalized video mixtapes with the look and feel of classic VHS tapes. We provide a desktop app, a browser extension for easy video capture, and a VR application to browse your collection in a virtual showroom.

Features Overview

Create & Share

Compile your favorite videos into mixtapes with custom labels and art.

Authentic VHS Feel

Experience nostalgic visual effects like tape wear and tracking issues.

VR Showroom

Browse and play your collection in an immersive virtual reality environment.

How It Works

  1. Capture

    Use our browser extension to grab videos from your favorite sites.

  2. Create

    Arrange your captured videos into a mixtape using our desktop app.

  3. Experience

    Immerse yourself in your collection with our VR showroom app.
